Who I Am

I am Nodjirambaye Penset Eric — an economist, educator, and aspiring AI researcher.

With over a decade of teaching experience, a BSc in Economics, and certifications in FLE and ESL from the British Council, I bring an interdisciplinary lens to the intersection of technology and development.

Currently pursuing an Associate Degree in Computer Science at UoPeople, with completed training in Data Science (AI Planet) and Responsible AI (Northeastern University).

Committed to leveraging technology and education for meaningful impact.

My current research focuses on how artificial intelligence shapes youth employment and digital divides in Nigeria — work that has been submitted to the OMEE journal and reflects my commitment to practitioner-informed, policy-relevant scholarship.

I believe deeply in the power of education as a tool for equity — particularly for African students navigating global scholarship and research opportunities.
