My Story

A journey across Chad, Nigeria, and the digital frontier — building bridges between research, technology, and social impact.

Who I Am

I am Nodjirambaye Penset Eric — an economist, educator, and aspiring AI researcher with roots in Chad and Nigeria. With over a decade of teaching experience, a BSc in Economics, and certifications in French language education (FLE) and ESL from the British Council, I bring a unique interdisciplinary lens to the intersection of technology and development.

My current research focuses on how artificial intelligence shapes youth employment and digital divides in Nigeria — work that has been submitted to the OMEE journal and reflects my commitment to practitioner-informed, policy-relevant scholarship.

I am also an advanced French speaker (Open University UK) and believe deeply in the power of education as a tool for equity — particularly for African students navigating global scholarship and research opportunities.
